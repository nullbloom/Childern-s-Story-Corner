The Brave Little Bunny

Once upon a time, in a peaceful meadow, there lived a little bunny named Benny. Benny was small and shy, and he was afraid of many things, especially the dark forest near his home.

One day, Benny’s best friend, a wise old owl, told him about a magical carrot hidden deep in the forest that could make anyone brave. Benny decided to be courageous and find the carrot.

As he hopped through the forest, Benny faced his fears. He met friendly animals who helped him along the way. When he finally found the glowing carrot, he took a big bite and felt a warm, brave feeling inside.

From that day on, Benny wasn’t afraid anymore. He explored the meadow and forest with confidence, making new friends and living happily ever after.

The End.
