The Lost Star

One clear night, a little star named Luma slipped from the sky and landed softly in a field where children played.

The children were amazed and decided to help Luma find her way back home. Together, they built a glowing ladder from their dreams and wishes.

With a final wave and sparkle, Luma climbed back up to the sky, promising to shine extra bright for her new friends below.

The End.
